Sanatan Shrivastava

Currently, I am pursuing my Master's in Computer Science (MSCS) at the University of Texas, Dallas (UTD). Previously, I completed my Bachelor of Technology in Computer Science and Engineering (CSE) from the Indian Institute of Information Technology (IIIT), Kota, India. During the last semester of my UG, I interned at Samsung Data Systems (SDS), India where I contributed to the overseas group in the development and delivery of software for American Region. Owing to my performance during the Internship, I was also offered a Full-Time Employment (FTE) as a Software Development Engineer - I (Senior Executive) at Samsung SDS India.

✨

Last year, I worked on a research project with Dr. Basant Agarwal and Dr. Sonal Jain where we leveraged the Machine Learning algorithms (SVM, AdaBoost, Random Forest, Logistic Regression etc) to find novel methodology of automating the diagnosis of a handwriting disorder - Dysgraphia. This led to the establishment of the 'Technology Interventions for Learning Disabilties' under the Ministry of Human Resources and Development (MHRD), Government of India.

In 2020-21, I worked with Dr. Priyanka Mishra on a research project that focused on automating standard wheelchairs for Quadraplegics using Artificial Intelligence of Things (AIoT). The goal was to increase the convenience with minimal human intervention.
