About me

I am a final year Computer Science undergraduate (B.Tech) student at the Indian Institute of Information Technology, Kota (IIIT Kota). Recently, I started working on a research project under the supervision of Dr. Basant Agrawal and Dr. Sonal Jain. Our work focuses on implementing several algorithms such as Adaptive Boosting Algorithm (adaBoost), Convoluted Neural Network (CNN) and others on handwriting samples to expedite the entire process diagnosis of Dysgraphia.

In summer of 2019, I worked on a research project under the mentorship of Dr. Priyanka Mishra The project was aimed at automating the conventional wheelchairs by leveraging Artificial Intelligence of Things (AIoT).

I am inclined towards designing systems and finding solutions to develop low latency Distributed Systems which are more accessible, and economic. Serverless Computing is another area of my interest, because of its utility in creating lightweight, robust, and cost-effective architecture. Working on these might result in development of next-generation computing infrastructure to make exisiting Healthcare, Education, commerical and other sectors more flourishing.

RESEARCH INTERESTS

Data Structures and Algorithms, Distributed Systems, Serverless Computing, Green Computing.
