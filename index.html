Biography

I am a final year Computer Science undergraduate (B.Tech) student at the Indian Institute of Information Technology, Kota (IIIT Kota). Recently, I worked on a research project under the supervision of Dr. Basant Agrawal and Dr. Sonal Jain. Our work focused on implementing several algorithms such as Support Vector Machine (SVM), Logistic Regression and others on handwriting samples to expedite the entire process diagnosis of Dysgraphia.

In the summer of 2019, I worked on a research project with Dr. Priyanka Mishra. The project was aimed at automating the conventional wheelchairs by leveraging Artificial Intelligence of Things (AIoT). I am inclined towards designing systems and finding solutions to develop low latency Distributed Systems which are more accessible, economic and environmentally sound. Serverless Computing is another area of my interest, because of its utility in creating lightweight, robust, and cost-effective architecture. Working on these might result in development of next-generation computing infrastructure to make exisiting Healthcare, Education, commerical and other sectors more flourishing.
